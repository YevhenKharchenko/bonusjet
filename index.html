About the Game

Bonusjet is a fast, colorful Match-3 puzzle game made for folks who like knowing what they're working toward, love those satisfying combo chains, and want their skills to actually pay off. Each level can be completed with 1–3 stars depending on performance. Achieving a full 3-star score not only boosts your rewards but also unlocks special hidden levels — unique mini-challenges that appear only for perfect players.
The game is free to download and play. Optional ad-supported features give boosters, extra lives, and energy refills.

How to Play

  • Match 3 or more tiles to clear the board.

  • Complete daily quests for bonuses.

  • Collect boosters for harder puzzles.

  • Earn up to 3 stars per level based on moves or score.

  • Progress through themed worlds and seasonal events.

  • Get all 3 stars and you'll unlock some hidden stages (trust us, they're worth it).
